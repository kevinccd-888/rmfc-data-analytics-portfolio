# ⚽ Royal Madrid FC: Strategic Recruitment & Financial Forecast

## 📊 Project Overview
This project presents a data-driven recruitment strategy and a multi-year economic outlook for a professional football club. Using **Tableau**, I developed an interactive visual analytics suite to identify elite replacement talent while strictly adhering to complex tactical, physical, and financial constraints.

**🔗 [View the Interactive Dashboard on Tableau Public] https://public.tableau.com/views/RMFC_Football_Analytics_Project/ScoutingTacticalDetails?:language=en-GB&:sid=&:redirect=auth&:display_count=n&:origin=viz_share_link)**

## 🎯 Key Business Objectives
* **Financial Efficiency:** Secure 4 to 6 elite players within a strictly capped $25,000,000 budget.
* **Tactical Fit:** Identify versatile targets with elite high-speed transition capabilities, high football intelligence, and dual-footedness to replace the injured main striker.
* **Economic Forecasting:** Project a 3-year wage trajectory incorporating a 5% compounded annual growth rate to ensure long-term fiscal health.

## 💡 Strategic Insights & Results
* **Budget Surplus Achieved:** Successfully acquired 4 elite targets with a total expenditure of $20,547,100. This secured a **17.81% budget surplus** ($4.45M), completely negating the need for the club's emergency overdraft allowance.
* **The "Hidden Cost" Barrier:** Data analysis revealed that non-sporting "hidden costs" (a 10% rival club premium and language integration fees) accounted for over $1.34M of the budget. Identifying this economic barrier allows the club to strategically pivot toward domestic markets to bypass artificial market inflation.

## 🛠️ Tools & Techniques
* **Data Visualization & Analytics:** Tableau Desktop
* **Techniques Used:** Calculated Fields, What-If Logic Modelling, Gestalt Principles, Progressive Disclosure (Tooltips).
* **Reporting:** Advanced Business Writing, Financial Forecasting.
